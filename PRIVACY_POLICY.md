# Privacy Policy – Kharcha Hisab Diary  
*Last Updated: November 17, 2025*

Kharcha Hisab Diary is a simple expense tracker and budget manager app for Android. We take your privacy very seriously.

### 1. Data We Collect
- Expense entries you add (amount, category, date, notes, payment method) → stored *only on your device* using local database (Hive)
- Optional Google Sign-In (only if you turn on Cloud Backup) → your data is saved to *your own Google account* (we never see it)
- Advertising ID (for showing ads via Google AdMob)
- Anonymous crash reports & usage stats (Google Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics)

### 2. Data We Do NOT Collect
- No bank details, no card numbers, no passwords
- No sensitive personal info (religion, health, etc.)
- We never sell, rent, or share your expense data with anyone

### 3. How We Use Your Data
- To show your expenses, reports, and reminders
- To backup & sync when you enable cloud backup
- To show ads (Google AdMob)
- To improve the app using anonymous analytics

### 4. Third-Party Services We Use
- Google AdMob → https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Authentication) → https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Sign-In & Drive Backup (only with your permission)

### 5. Ads & Personalization
You can turn off personalized ads anytime:  
Phone Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

### 6. Data Deletion
- Delete all expenses: Settings → Clear All Data
- Remove cloud backup: Sign out from the app
- Full data deletion request: Email us at Kharchahisabdiary247@gmail.com

### 7. Security
Your data is stored locally and (if backed up) encrypted in your own Google account. We follow industry-standard security practices.

### 8. Children’s Privacy
This app is not for children under 13 years. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

### 9. Changes to Policy
We will notify you inside the app if we make big changes.

### 10. Contact Us
Any questions? Email: Kharchahisabdiary247@gmail.com

This privacy policy complies with Google Play requirements and India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023.
